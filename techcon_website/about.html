History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots initiative by a small group of passionate technologists who envisioned a collaborative platform for sharing cutting-edge ideas. Held initially in a modest community hall, the first conference focused primarily on open-source technologies and community-driven development.

Over the years, TechCon rapidly expanded its scope and reach, attracting participants and speakers from around the globe. By 2015, it had become a prominent event in the tech calendar, recognized for its forward-thinking discussions on AI, blockchain, cybersecurity, and sustainable tech. Each annual conference has built upon the last, fostering a legacy of innovation and knowledge exchange.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to be the leading catalyst for technological advancement and human connection within the global tech community. We are driven by the following core principles:

  • Empowerment: To provide attendees with the knowledge, tools, and connections they need to innovate and succeed.
  • Collaboration: To foster an ecosystem where ideas are freely exchanged, leading to groundbreaking partnerships and projects.
